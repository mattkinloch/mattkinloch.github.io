You Got "Lucky"

What is "luck"? Is there a small little green leperchaun that prances around the end of a rainbow sprinkling magical dust around the world making people lucky? Is it merely an invention of salty schoolground children used to explain why the got fucking bodied by Kevin at first base in kickball? Probably somewhere in between. For our intents and purpose, we'll define a "lucky" win as scoring worse than the rest of the league in a week, but still managing to walk away with a big fat "W". An "unlucky" loss mean a team scored better than most of the league that week, but still managed to lose. To calculate this, I'm going to take the average score across the league for each week then compare all of the losing team's scores to it to determine who was the "unluckiest", and vice versa for the "luckiest".
