You Got "Lucky"

I think there's been some misunderstanding as to what this section is. Our lovable little boy Smuth asked me who I was choosing for unluckiest of the week, but I don't get to choose. It's all about the numbers baby. If someone scores in the upper half and still loses by being unluckily matched up against one of the few teams who scored higher, that's our definition of unlucky. Vice versa for luckiest. Your performance has absolutely no impact on your opponents, so a huge part in whether you walk away with an L or W is whoever you were randomly matched up against. Fantasy is like putting two dudes in a room, blindfolding them, and having them jer- nevermind.

Boston Weathermen was the luckiest team this week, scoring third lowest with 123.8 points but playing against the second lowest scorer Joe Biden's Speechwriter who posted 110.55 points. Why'd you have to start someone who was designated OUT, buddy? Why? Especially why against the Weathermen? SMH. You were so close to glory and you blew it.

Our unluckiest team of the week was a special little team that was bound to show up here eventually. You guessed it, it's me. Scrub Daddy scored 3rd highest with 146.3 points, but got absolutely rocked by MNF and the Wuhan Bats. An honorable mention, because I would hate to hog the spotlight, is female playing, who scored 4th highest and was matched up against the 2nd highest scoring team Earl Thomas' Brother. The four top scores bunched up into only two games? It's almost impossible to get unluckier than that. Absolutely brutal for us this week.
